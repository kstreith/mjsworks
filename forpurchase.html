Artists Loft at Wake Forest Coffee Shop

My encaustic paintings and photos are available for purchase at Wake Forest Artists Loft, this is on the second floor of the Wake Forest Coffee Shop. Wake Forest Coffee Shop is located at 156 S. White Street Wake Forest, North Carolina

Pricing

  • 6x6 Encaustic Painting - $50
  • 8x10 Encaustic Painting - $80-$125

Encaustic Workshops

I occassionally teach encaustic workshops at the Artists Loft at the Wake Forest Coffee Shop. You can find the current class schedule at the Wake Forest Artists Loft website. I teach both an beginners class and an intermediate class.

Commissioned Work

Feel free to send an e-mail to info@mjsworks.com for more details on encaustic painting commissions. I can incorporate photos as parts of the final piece.

By Mail

If none of the above work for you, just send me an e-mail to info@mjsworks.com and we can discuss mailing my work directly to you.
